Gender makeup of Vail Resorts board of directors

Since 2013, at least one director on Vail Resorts' board has been a woman, and its current board outdoes most other public companies in the state in terms of board gender diversity. Only five other publicly traded companies headquartered in Colorado have a higher percentage of women on their boards of directors, according to data from the Women's Leadership Council as of June 30, 2021.
